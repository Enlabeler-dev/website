Operations and marketing manager

Cape Town · Full time

Ask a question Apply

Want to work with us?

At Enlabeler, we’re always looking to hire ambitious people to join our rapidly growing team of operators, labelers and support staff. We partner with Cape AI to provide our team with opportunities for growth and cross-learning in the data industry. Together we aim to grow the next generation of data professionals in South Africa. We’ve established other partnerships across the board - from social impact organizations to AI community groups and companies who share the purpose of fighting youth unemployment.

What we’re looking for

Are you a self-starter who likes to build and operate the Enlabeler community? Do you have a hands-on mentality and enjoy working with people? We’re looking for a highly-responsible individual who’s keen to enter this exciting start-up and oversee the total data labeling operations. Ability to take on a variety of tasks, from intake meetings with clients to guide/develop/train the community of data labelers.

Responsibilities

The day-to-day operations of the labeling teams. Making sure labeling teams are treated appropriately and cared for

The deployment of the mobile platform. We expect you to take the lead in furthering the development of our labeling platform

Assisting with tasks such as following-up on client queries and the planning of internal/external organizational get-togethers and events

Brand strategy development for Enlabeler and update our marketing channels (Website, LinkedIn page, Facebook community page, Google and Instagram)

The Operations & Marketing manager will have to be present on the labeling locations in Phillipi / Khayelitsha 50%-100% of their time


Applicant requirements

You've completed Matric Certificate, followed by a tertiary degree or diploma. You've been working for 3-5 years and are looking for a new challenge

You live in Phillippi, Khayelitsha, the surrounding area, and/or don't mind working in those areas for the majority of your time

We're looking for a team player! You need to be a good communicator who has strong social skills, and the ability to naturally connect with people

Self-starter. Full of initiative and ideas. You don't mind diving into operational tasks, and can easily keep oversight and organize yourself

An advanced understanding of isiXhosa, and ideally able to understand other SA languages as well

Drivers license is a plus, but not essential


Compensation

Depending on experience.
