Partnerships that help us scale

Enlabeler is open to collaborate with different companies and organizations in the field, who share the same mission.

Enlabeler is a start-up that serves one sole purpose: Create low-entry data labeling jobs in South-African townships and African communities all over the continent. In the true spirit of Ubuntu (I am because We are), we can only do this work by forming valuable partnerships. These partnerships include government bodies, social impact organizations (in the area of youth development and unemployment) and companies that operate in the same industry as we do and share our vision of an AI ready Africa. If you would like to collaborate with us, please reach out.
