Values

We’re constantly hiring the
most ambitious people in South Africa.

We are looking for people who can bring new perspectives and life experiences to our teams. We value curiosity, passion, the desire to learn and big thinkers that are doers. We are committed to equal employment opportunity regardless of race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, age, citizenship, disability or gender identity.

A key differentiator

We are committed to provide our labelers on-the-job learning opportunities..

Experience what it is like to join the economic workforce and learn about the data labeling industry. We provide training, but most importantly offer you a spot into our community of data labelers - for you to learn and grow, and start your career.

Exciting projects

Provide purposeful work on projects that have a positive impact

State-of-the-art technology

Exposure to modern technologies in a low-entry way

Focus on your development

We are a stepping stone for labelers to enter the modern, tech-savvy workforce and get hands-on work experience

Fun

We create a fun and connected community of labelers who can work remotely via our platform

Application process

1

Apply.

We'll review your CV, motivation letter and invite you to fill out a questionnaire.

2

Interview.

The last step of the process is an interview aimed at assessing your professional maturity, as well as the goodness of fit between yourself and our team at Enlabeler.

3

Grow.

From your first day at the job we help you develop yourself rapidly.

Happy labelers are our highest priority.

We ensure a hands-on learning environment for
you to enter the workforce, and enable you to have a positive impact on people and planet.
