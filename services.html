If you want to have better insights into the behaviour of your customers then it’s time to start annotating your unstructured data. If your looking to develop a computer vision model or improve your chatbot, your digital workforce needed for the future, it’s time for us humans, to take a look at your data to learn your machines.

Get in touch What is data labeling?

On premise

Enlabeler has physical locations available where teams can work in a controlled environment, within the client’s interface of your choice. They can do the data annotation or labeling work within the client systems environment for easy integration with your processes. This is especially great for privacy-sensitive data sets that have a consistent flow and can be planned easily.

Mobile platform

Our mobile app offering is easily scalable and works with on-demand contractual labelers. It’s easily scalable and incredibly flexible. Its best used for: non-personal data, fluctuating, peak / low scale moments

At the client

Enlabeler can also provide the client with dedicated teams of young talented people who work within your office space. We can help select and train those candidates that meet the client profile - and they’ll be able to do the data labeling work for you, being contracted by Enlabeler.

Intake process

In order for the Enlabeler team to give you a fast and reliable response on your dataset, we would need the below:

1

Sample.

A dataset must consist of a representative sample. This can be sent to us in any format.

2

Goal.

The goal and task to be performed must be known.

3

Instruction.

A set of instructions for the labelers about how to apply the labels to your data

Quality assurance

Our quality assurance processes guarantee the quality you expect.

Enlabeler ensures that the quality of the work is according to the client's standards. We provide cross-validation before sending the datasets back to the client and we test our labelers before giving a new task. Per labeling job we agree together with you on quality meausure and score.
1)
2)

Measure

We tailor the quality measure with you (e.g. F1 score)

Score

We agree together about the expected score (e.g. F1>0.85)

Ground truth

We score performance of labelers by including labeled images in the dataset.

Optimize

Depending on the task we can have data redundantly labeled and we optimize this process for you.
